How Rentlyhood Works

How to Rent?

  • Search for the item on Rentlyhood app
  • Choose dates and submit the booking request
  • Make payment once lender approves booking request
  Watch Tutorial

How to Lend?

  • In lender profile of Rentlyhood app, click 'Create New Listing'
  • Enter details of item and upload photos
  • Submit for Approval
  Watch Tutorial
