Tips to prevent accidents:

Prevention is better than cure. Therefore, here are some tips that can help prevent damage:

  • Ask and explain how to use the product. In most cases damage is not caused intentionally, but due to unintentionally incorrect use of a product
  • Treat someone else's belongings as you would your own
  • Make clear agreements about what you expect from each other via Rentlyhood in-app chat
  • Before and after the rental, take good quality pictures of the item so that you have proof of the items rented, in case of any dispute
  • Sharing is based on mutual reasonableness , trust and respect
