Nature of the Service

The Service allows users to rent from, and lend goods ("Loaned Items") to other users. Use of the Service may require a compatible mobile device/laptop and/or access to a data network. Third party data use fees may apply.


How renting works

  • Users register for the Service and find an item for rent listed by an Owner/lender.
  • The Rentee may then request to rent the item from the Owner/Lender for a specified time period, mentioned pick-up location and calculated price shown on the app.
  • Based upon the requested date, Lender may approve or reject rentee’s request. Once approved, rentee and lender may use in-app chat to further align/agree on any other details.
  • Rentee would then proceed ahead with the payment on the app.
  • On rental start date, rentee should pick-up items from Lender from the mentioned pick-up location. Unless otherwise agreed by Owner/lender and Rentee in writing via in-app chat, all rented items are to be picked up and returned during the rental period specified on the website/app.

All rented items must be returned at or before the time scheduled. Failure to return a rented item in a timely manner shall be a violation of these Terms of Service and shall result in the imposition of a late fee. Repeated or prolonged failure to return a rented item or items in a timely manner, in addition to grounds for the imposition of late fees, shall be a violation of these Terms of Service, grounds for termination of the user’s account, and may be reported to law enforcement as possible theft.


Late fee Charges

Rentals returned late as per the schedule on the site, or after the previously agreed upon time to return the item, are subject to a late fee as follows (unless agreed with lender in written via in-app chat):

  • Items returned late up to 2 hours* but same day as due: 20% of the daily rental charge
  • Items returned late by more than 2 hours* but same day as due: 100% of the daily rental charge
  • Items returned late and not on the same day as due: 110% of the daily rental charge per day

*Hours calculations follow the 24 hour principle from the time of pick-up. For example, If Rentee has picked-up an item on 21st March 10:00 AM for 2 days then the item has to returned by 23rd March 10:00 AM

This guarantee is there to ensure that renting out and lending out items can be done safely and without any worries. So make sure you name all the items when you make the appointment via the Rentlyhood chat. For example: if you lend a game console with two controllers to your neighbor, but your advertisement says it is one, name that extra controller in the chat;
  • check the items together when returning them and report damage no later than 48 hours after the end of the rental or loan. Take photos of the damage and a description and email it to “contact@rentlyhood.com”; and
  • Is your product insured or do you still have a warranty with the store? Then you should first appeal to them for the same.

  • everything that does not fall under the heading of consumer products, so no food, leftover paint, plants, etc;
  • vans, passenger cars, motorcycles and other motorized means of transport;
  • property;
  • clothing cleaning costs;
  • foodstuffs; and
  • drugs, weapons and other illegal products.
The following situations are excluded from the Rentlyhood guarantee
  • lost income: (rental) income that you miss out on due to damage or loss of your product;
  • cosmetic damage: we can only pay out the guarantee in case of such damage that it impedes the function of the object;

  • damage or theft outside the rental or loan period; and

We will review the damage and let you know whether or not it qualifies for the Rentlyhood warranty. In some cases we may ask you for additional information. Does your application for guarantee not comply with the rules or is there a suspicion of fraud? Then Rentlyhood reserves the right not to apply the warranty or only partially. If your report is in good faith and complies with the rules, you can in most cases assume that the guarantee will be granted.
